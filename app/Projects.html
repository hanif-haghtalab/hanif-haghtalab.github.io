Distorted Reality Distorted RealityDistorted Reality

AmberNetworkFestival 2021 Berlin

For a long time, the boundary between reality and imagination, between what is in our minds and what we see before our eyes, was explicitly recognizable for us. And the means we used to recognize this boundary were our senses and perception. Gradually, the media became so powerful that could have created another world for us in which our senses were teased in different ways. The diverse mass media and simulated worlds have cast doubt on whether what we have known so far, is the true reality? We want to know how the mass media affect our understanding of reality and hasn't the reality been distorted from the very beginning? Here we break down all our perceptions of reality in order to make some space for the presence of reality itself by creating holes. we aim to find another reality behind our current hyper-reality. The reality that in itself is another hyper-reality because the media changes it in any case. Music:Omid Zolfaghari Visual:Hanif Haghtalab More info

Sang e Siah Hotel Sang e Siah HotelSang e Siah Hotel

Iran Shiraz

This project was defined with the aim of creating a traditional residence in the historical context of Shiraz, Sang-e-Siah neighborhood, between five National Registered Buildings from Ilkhani, Zandeih, Qajar and Pahlavi historical periods. In recent years, contextual rehabilitation projects have been developed based on the demolition and aggregation strategies, which gradually eliminate the proportions of void and mass, the geometric and communication system of passages and the structure of a significant part of historical context by eradicating fine-grained structures and passages, creating large-scale projects and constructing streets.
